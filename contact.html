Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is NEXUS Store?

NEXUS Store is a next-generation e-commerce platform offering cutting-edge products with 3D visualization, AI-powered recommendations, and immersive shopping experiences.

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Account" button in the top navigation, then select "Register". Fill out the required information and verify your email address to complete the process.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. We never share your data with third parties without your consent.

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide shipping information and payment details to complete your order.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, please contact our customer support team for assistance.

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days), express shipping (2-3 business days), and overnight shipping. Free shipping is available on orders over $50.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 150 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original condition with all packaging and accessories included.

How do I initiate a return?

Log into your account, go to "My Orders", find the item you want to return, and click "Return Item". Follow the instructions to print a return label.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link.

How do I update my account information?

Log into your account and go to "Profile Settings" where you can update your personal information, addresses, and preferences.
